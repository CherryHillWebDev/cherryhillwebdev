About Cherry Hill Web Dev

CUSTOM WEBSITES, BUILT FOR YOU

A Small Business Web Developer Who Gets Your Needs

I’m Matt, the founder of Cherry Hill Web Dev. Over around two years I taught myself to code websites by hand, so that I can spend more time with my family as my wife and I begin to think about expanding. I started offering my services to local businesses near me, and I have come to really love working with small business owners. Most small businesses can’t afford to pay thousands of dollars upfront for a website, and off-the-shelf solutions like Squarespace or Wordpress don’t provide the care or results they deserve.

My $150/month plan is designed to make custom websites accessible, sustainable, and effective for small business owners and individual sellers. My clients have seen huge growth after working with me, because a hand-coded site will always rank higher than an off-the-shelf site. I work with each business owner individually to create sites that are tailored to their needs, which generate more leads and traffic than any site-builder can do.

OUR PROCESS

Here's What You Can Expect

Our structured process is designed create a site that you're completely happy with the first time, every time. We guide you through each step with clear communication, thoughtful feedback, and no surprises along the way.

Design

First we’ll talk about your business, your needs, and what you need your site to do. I’ll give you some design options and you’ll choose which you like.

Step 1

Develop

I’ll build the site out and send you a link for you to review it. Here, you can request any changes you want, to make sure you’re 100% satisfied.

Step 2

Deploy

Now the site is live! After it’s online, you can send me any updates you want, and I’ll make those changes to the site within 5 business days.

Step 3

After we agree to work together, we’ll have a conversation about your business, the type of clients you have and want, what you want your website to look like, and what you want your website to do for your business. I’ll show you some design ideas and you’ll be able to choose which you like, and make any special requests about the site.

After it’s built, you can review it and approve your new website which I’ll send you via a private link. Here’s your opportunity to make sure it looks the way you want, and works the way you want as well. Then, once you’re happy with it, I’ll make the site live and manage any updates you send me (such as uploading new pictures or copy).
